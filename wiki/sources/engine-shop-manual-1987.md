### Intake Manifold

- Two-piece aluminum casting (upper + lower)
- Runner lengths tuned for torque/power
- Provides mounting for air throttle body, fuel supply manifold, EGR valve/tube, vacuum taps
- Injector pockets machined to prevent air/fuel leakage
- Manifold bolt torque: 32–33 N·m (23–25 ft-lb) in sequence; wait 10 minutes, then retorque

### Crankcase Ventilation

- Closed-type positive crankcase ventilation system
- PCV valve located at rear of upper intake manifold (5.0L EFI)

### Fuel System – EFI (Section 24-05)

- **Type:** Multi-point, pulse time, EFI system (8 injectors)
- **Injector firing:** Two banks of 4, fired alternately once per crank revolution
- **Fuel delivery:** Two-pump system:
  - Low pressure in-tank pump (inlet at bottom of internal sump)
  - High pressure chassis/frame rail mounted pump: 269.0 kPa (39 psi), 60 L/hr (15.9 gal/hr)
- **Overpressure relief:** 850 kPa (123 psi)
- **Fuel pump control:** EEC-IV module via fuel pump relay; pump shuts off if engine below 120 rpm
- **Key-on pre-pressurization:** Pump runs ~1 second then shuts off if ignition not turned to START
- **Inertia switch location (F-Series):** Toe-board, to the right of the transmission hump
- **Inertia switch reset:** Depress button on top of switch; never reset until fuel system inspected for leaks
- **Fuel pressure regulator:** Part 9C968, attached to fuel supply manifold upstream of injectors, vacuum-referenced; returns excess fuel to tank
- **Injector O-rings:** Two per injector; inspect at service
- **Injector "plastic hat":** Covers pintle; inspect at service; if missing, check intake manifold

### Ignition System (Section 23-01)

- **System:** TFI-IV (Thick Film Integration IV) with EEC-IV
- **Distributor:** Hall effect vane switch; PIP (Profile Ignition Pickup) sensor provides crankshaft position to TFI module and EEC-IV
- **TFI module function:** Supplies voltage to PIP; controls dwell; limits primary current; passes timing signal to EEC-IV (SPOUT)
- **Spark plugs:** 14mm, 1/2-inch standard reach, tapered seat design
- **Timing adjustments:** On TFI-IV systems, timing is not a normal field adjustment — controlled by EEC-IV. Only spark plug gap and octane adjustment apply.
- **If SPOUT signal open:** Module defaults to base timing using PIP signal
- **Spark plug gap/timing:** Refer to engine emissions control decal for vehicle-specific specs

### Cooling System (Thermostat)

- Starts to open: 100–107°C (180–192°F)
- Fully open: 111–118°C (200–212°F)
- Not adjustable; replace if out of range

### Fuel Type
