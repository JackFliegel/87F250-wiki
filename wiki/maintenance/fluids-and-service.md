## Known Notes

- **Oil fill is 6 qt with filter change** — do not overfill; check dipstick. Source: Engine Shop Manual §21-01
- **Spark plug gap and timing specs are on the engine decal** — always refer to the decal on this specific vehicle's rocker arm cover before adjusting or replacing plugs. Source: Engine Shop Manual §23-01
